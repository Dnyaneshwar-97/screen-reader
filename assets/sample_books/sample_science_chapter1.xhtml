Chapter 1: What is Science?

Science is a way of learning about the natural world. Scientists observe, ask questions, and conduct experiments to find answers.

1.1 The Scientific Method

The scientific method is a step-by-step process used by scientists to investigate phenomena and acquire new knowledge.

  1. Make an observation
  2. Ask a question
  3. Form a hypothesis
  4. Conduct an experiment
  5. Analyze the results
  6. Draw a conclusion

1.2 Why Science Matters

Science helps us understand the world around us. From medicine to technology, scientific discoveries improve our daily lives.

Field Example Discovery
Biology Understanding how cells work
Physics Understanding how light travels
Chemistry Understanding how substances react

Remember: curiosity is the foundation of all scientific inquiry.
